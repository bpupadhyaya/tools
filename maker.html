Maker

Describe what you want — Maker builds a real, running tool for it, entirely on your machine. The AI-age successor to make.

Download View source

What it does

🗣️ Build by talking

Say "build me a tip calculator" — a working tool appears, and you refine it by poking at it.

🧩 Many tools, remembered

Every build is its own tool. Reopen, iterate, undo, and compose — Maker keeps your history.

👁️ Reads images

Paste a screenshot and Maker sees it — routed to a local vision model automatically.

🔒 Asks before touching files

Permission-gated read/write to your folders — nothing happens without your OK.

🧠 Your model, your rules

Runs open-source models locally (llama.cpp / MLX). Optionally connect a cloud key for the hard 20%.

💻 GUI and terminal

A full browser workshop or a fast terminal — same engine, your choice.
