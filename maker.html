Maker

Describe what you want — Maker builds a real, running tool for it, entirely on your machine. The AI-age successor to make.

Get Maker free View source

What it does

🗣️ Build by talking

Say "build me a tip calculator" — a working tool appears, and you refine it by poking at it.

🧩 Many tools, remembered

Every build is its own tool. Reopen, iterate, undo, and compose — Maker keeps your history.

👁️ Reads images

Paste a screenshot and Maker sees it — routed to a local vision model automatically.

🔒 Asks before touching files

Permission-gated read/write to your folders — nothing happens without your OK.

🧠 Your model, your rules

Runs open-source models locally (llama.cpp / MLX). Optionally connect a cloud key for the hard 20%.

💻 GUI, terminal — even your phone

A full browser workshop or a fast terminal, same engine. maker serve --lan opens it from your phone on the same Wi-Fi.

Get Maker

Two commands from a fresh checkout to a running workshop. Needs Node ≥ 23.6 and git.

git clone https://github.com/bpupadhyaya/maker && cd maker
node packages/gui/serve.ts

That opens the workshop at 127.0.0.1:4319. Click ⛁ Models → Download to fetch a local model (~1 GB to start) — the one time it needs internet. Want a maker command and an app icon? Run bash scripts/install.sh (or install.ps1 on Windows).
